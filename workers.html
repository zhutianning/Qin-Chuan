2017, Summer, Shaoxing

It it one of my series of portraits for construction workers in my hometown, during the summer, 2017. At that summer, the main street of the city was refurbished, taking 2 entire months for the 2 kilometres' long street. I made this documentary collection for these aliens, who even haven't been to this city before but eventually shaped the face of my hometown. They had gone silently, only a few of photos left.
