This is a
Unknown Planet

This is a blind area, because I can never see ......

This is a blind area, because I can never see the back of my own head. It was only because someone else told me that I learned that I started showing sparse patches of white hair when I was only 20 years old, so I would look anxiously in the mirror, trying to see the back of my head, and even cover it up with a hat. For that reason, I soon became interested in observing others, and curious to see how they think about this realm that cannot be seen with their own eyes, so I used a close-up image to explore the whorls of heads and feel every strand of hair breathing in the sun, depicting its lines with the camera.

At this time, I was shocked by my obsession, eventually realized that there was in fact no need to hide. Under the sun, the white hair is nothing but a part of me, a mysterious unknown. Under the lens, the backside of head is a crazy growth of vegetation or a barren planet, lonely but unique, exuding a charming light.
