LEAF AND GRASS PORTRAIT

The square-cut photographs as seen are portraits I took in Taiwan at different time and places. These photos are but a small part of the series photos of people I met when I went out. I enjoy the moment of having eye contacts with others. So I would step forward actively to require for taking a picture of the people who have eye contacts with me, through which, I could create and initiate a conversation with others. In the brief conversations, those strangers whom I never met before, however, revealed the most friendly and sincere smiles indeed. Even some people did not want me to take their pictures and refused my request tactfully, they still said sorry to me.

Photo Gallery

Therefore, gradually, all these photos have converged into a connection network of the city. People do not know each other, yet instead of the habitual indifferences of the city, what I have perceived is friendliness and the expectation for communications.
Every single image is about a unique individual. Nonetheless, the kindness of humanity is preserved in everyone, which touches me. Owing to this project, it enables me to piece together an ideal and harmonious world where my anxious gaze gains responses, recognition, and comfort.
In the city, we are the long-lost family. With such a dream, I send out an invitation to everyone with photos.
